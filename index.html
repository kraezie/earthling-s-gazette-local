Paku Youth Joins Scrap Selling for DYD 2023

By Patrecia Ceelyn Sombito



Paku youth joined and participated the scrap selling as part of the fund-raising activity in preparation for the DYD (Diocesan Youth Day) to be held at ICP Baybay parish, Baybay City, Leyte on July 20-23, 2023.

Youth President, Aeron Jester Delima initiated the said activity, they went to different houses to collect used appliances like washing machine and electric fan. They also managed to collect plastic bottles, metal, cans and sell it to scrap sellers. The money of this scrap selling will be the proceeds to DYD 2023 activity which is intended for registration fees, parish t-shirt, immersion kit, food and accommodation.

"Ako mapasalamaton sa injong dakong supporta/tabang kanamo, dako na kining tabang alang kanamo", Aeron said in an interview.

Scrap selling is more than just an activity for the said fund raising, it is a calling for us people specially youth to take good care of our nature and practice to sustainability. Plastic bottles, trash, and aluminum metal should be disposed properly or might as well recycle it if possible. In addition, by recycling scrap metal, people can reduce production cost, save energy and help the environment.

Lastly, recycling helps reduce landfill pollution, raw materials and fossil fuel consumption and generates a circular economy. Thus, this youth empowerment is important asconcerning environment challenges because after all this is our nature, and our home.
